FAQs

What is cloud computing?

Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services—such as servers, storage, databases, networking, software, and analytics—over the internet (“the cloud”). It offers faster innovation, flexible resources, and economies of scale.

How can machine learning benefit my business?

Machine learning can provide valuable insights from data, predict trends, and automate processes. This can lead to better decision-making, improved customer experiences, and enhanced efficiency in business operations.

What are the advantages of migrating to the cloud?

Cloud migration can provide numerous benefits, including reduced IT costs, improved scalability, enhanced security, and increased flexibility. It allows businesses to focus on their core competencies while leveraging advanced cloud services.
